Hello world.

My name is Ondřej, I’m 23 years old and building my career in digital marketing and multimedia. I come from Žatec, a charming town northwest of Prague inscribed in UNESCO world heritage list. Currently, I finished my studies in multimedia at the Faculty of Economics and Administration in Pardubice. Over the years, I’ve gained diverse experience from my first role as an usher at the Summer Cinema, to spending four years at the tourist complex Temple of Hops and Beer. Today, I manage social media and create websites for small businesses and organizations, helping them strengthen their online presence. MORE
